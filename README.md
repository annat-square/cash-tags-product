# Cash Tags & Project Mint — Product Resources

Product documentation and requirements for Physical Cash Tags (Tags) and Project Mint.

## What are Tags?

Tags are physical NFC-enabled payment devices (wands, mini cards, etc.) that customers can order through the Cash App. They are powered by the Taply integration and surfaced through the Whimsicard service.

## What is Project Mint and how is it related to Tags?

Project Mint encapsulates all critical functionality for Tags, in addition to a new card ordering experience. Critical functionality for Tags is defined by anything necessary to ordering, activating, and transacting with a Tag. Our new card ordering experience ('Card Design Library') includes new card customization tools, partnership/edition cards, and a new payment form factor selection process ('Grid).
